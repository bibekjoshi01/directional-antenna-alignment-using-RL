\subsection{Objectives}

The objectives of this project are:
\begin{itemize}
    \item To design and fabricate a low-cost receiver platform capable of measuring RSSI in real time and mechanically steering a directional antenna in azimuth and tilt
    \item To develop embedded firmware that performs RSSI acquisition, signal conditioning, state encoding, actuator control, and experiment logging on the ESP32-S3
    \item To formulate and deploy a lightweight tabular Q-learning policy that can execute on the embedded platform without online training
    \item To compare the deployed reinforcement learning approach against baseline alignment methods, namely fixed orientation, exhaustive scanning, and RSSI-based hill-climbing, using consistent evaluation metrics
\end{itemize}
