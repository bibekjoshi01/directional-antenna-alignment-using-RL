\section{IMPLEMENTATION DETAILS}

The final implementation of the proposed autonomous antenna alignment system combines
mechanical actuation, embedded RSSI acquisition, offline reinforcement learning, and
field validation within a single end-to-end workflow. The implementation was carried
out in four tightly connected stages: hardware fabrication, firmware development,
offline model training, and practical experimentation.

At the hardware level, a two degree-of-freedom positioning mechanism was developed to rotate the
receiving directional antenna in pan and tilt while preserving stable power and signal
routing across the rotating interface. At the embedded level, the ESP32-S3 was used
as the central controller for packet reception, RSSI extraction, servo and stepper
actuation, and policy execution.
